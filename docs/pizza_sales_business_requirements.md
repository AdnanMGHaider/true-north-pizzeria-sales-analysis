# True North Pizzeria Sales Analysis

## Business Requirements Document

### 1. Project Background

True North Pizzeria wants to evaluate its one year of sales performance to guide decision making across marketing, menu optimization, product strategy, and operational planning.

### 2. Project Objectives

- Measure core sales KPIs
- Understand sales distribution by category and size
- Identify best and worst selling pizza items
- Analyze daily and monthly order patterns
- Provide insights for marketing, inventory, and staffing decisions

### 3. Data Source

- Dataset Name: **pizza_sales_raw.csv**
- Total rows in dataset: **48621 (including header row)**

- Key fields:
  - pizza_id
  - order_id
  - pizza_name_id
  - quantity
  - order_date
  - order_time
  - unit_price
  - total_price
  - pizza_size
  - pizza_category
  - pizza_ingredients
  - pizza_name

### 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Pizzas Sold
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Average Pizza per Order

### 5. Business Questions

- What is the overall sales performance of True North Pizzeria?
- How do sales vary across pizza categories and sizes?
- Which pizzas are the top and bottom performers?
- What are the daily sales patterns?
- What are the monthly sales patterns?
- When are the busiest ordering periods?

### 6. Deliverables

- Power BI dashboard (Home page and Best Worst Sellers page)
- SQL validation queries
- SQL validation report (Word or PDF)
- Final stakeholder ready README
